Climate Data Analysis

Applications

Ample amounts of data is collected on a daily basis that can be used to assess modern consequences of a changing climate and human-altered environments. Some examples include satellite imagery of a changing agricultural landscape, sea ice changes in the Arctic, human-health implications and even quantify the response of the general public on climate change via social media.
